Las Enchiladas

What is a Enchilada?

An enchilada consists of a corn tortilla that is rolled around a filling and then covered with a savory sauce. Fillings can include meats such as chicken, beef, or pork, as well as cheese, beans, potatoes, vegetables, or seafood. The sauce is a key component and may be chili-based like salsa roja, tomato-based like salsa verde, or cheese-based such as chile con queso. Some modern variations use flour tortillas, which are softer and can hold more ingredients, though corn tortillas are traditional.
