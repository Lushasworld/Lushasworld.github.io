A 3D image is a digitally created representation that simulates a three-dimensional appearance, allowing viewers to perceive objects as if they exist in real space. Unlike traditional 2D images, 3D images incorporate depth, enhancing realism and providing varied perspectives. They are often created using advanced computer graphics techniques, capturing intricate details such as lighting and texture, and are commonly used in fields like animation, video games, and marketing.
